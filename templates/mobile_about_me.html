About Me

I'm at a turning point in my life and have now decided to follow my passion and pursue software engineering as a career. Outside of my current day job as purchasing manager, I try to spend as much time as I can learning and practising coding by conducting research and applying these new skills to projects I'm working on.

In my free time you'll find me in the gym, trying out a new restaurant or petting all the dogs.
